How to Play Multisweeper

The goal of multisweeper is to claim the highest number of tiles possible until all non-bomb tiles are claimed at which point the player with the highest score wins.

Tiles operate under the following rules:

  • A tile has a 20% chance of being a bomb.
  • The count of each tile is the sumof each bomb directly touching, including diagonal to, it.
  • You can only see the count of the tiles you have claimed.

The game is best played with a mouse, a keyboard is optional.

WASD, Arrow Keys, or clicking and dragging with the mouse can move the camera.

Flags can be placed to remind yourself where you believe a bomb may be, outside of that they have no effect.
